Bunny Hop
Action Key
Jump (1P) ↑
Move Left (2P) A / ←
Move Right (2P) D / →
Bunny Hop was the first game that I developed upon enrolling into my University and realising that there were various arcade machines in one of our buildings!

It was a great way for me to get back into the process of working on and sharing games! While it's quite simple and concise, I feel it's a great game that came out more polished then I initially expected and always makes for a quick bit of fun before lectures!

My girlfriend also provided the bunny sprites, which gave me some valuable experience in directing/working with someone else to make a game more appealing!
